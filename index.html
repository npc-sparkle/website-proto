The Sweetest Angel

About

The Sweetest Angel was born and brought up in Dresden, Germany. His interest in music from early years on made him go to the evangelic Gymnasium des Heiligen Kreuzes. There he received singing education and sang in the famous Dresdener Engelschor. He went on international tours with the choir but also gave his first concerts as a soloist. In 2010 he received the first place in the national competition "Putti musizieren". The Sweetest Angel alrady sang in Asia, South America and the United States. He is a fellow of Life Music Now.
