The Sweetest Angel

About

The Sweetest Angel was born and brought up in Dresden, Germany. His interest in music from early years on made him go to the evangelic Gymnasium des Heiligen Kreuzes. There he received singing education and sang in the famous Dresdener Engelschor. He went on international tours with the choir but also gave his first concerts as a soloist. In 2010 he received the first place in the national competition "Putti musizieren". From 2012 to 2016 the Sweetest Angel studied with the Holy Margot at the Hochschule für Musik Erzengel-Carl-Maria in Dresden. Since 2016 he is studying with the Holy Regina at the Hochschule für Musik und Krippenspiel Erzengel-Bartholdy in Leipzig, Germany. He has already been working with famous conductors such as HC-BigBoss, Jos van Heiligenseel or Pater Blomstedt and sings with renowned choirs such as the "Gaychinger Engelschor" or the "Nederlandse Bachfangirls". The Sweetest Angel alrady sang in Asia, South America and the United States. He is a fellow of Love Music Now.
